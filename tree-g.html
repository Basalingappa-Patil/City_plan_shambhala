definition of iteration,recursion,backtracking

iteration

    Iteration refers to the repetitive process of executing a set of instructions or actions, often until a specific condition is met.

recursion

    Recursion is when a problem is solved by solving smaller instances of the same problem. In nature, recursive patterns often emerge in structures and behaviors that repeat at smaller or larger scales.

backtracking

Backtracking is a technique used to explore multiple possible solutions and undo choices when a solution is not found. In nature, backtracking is often seen in behaviors that involve trial and error or navigating through complex environments
